# 2. Offers, Packages, and Pricing

## The core rule

Do not sell “AI agents.” Sell a business outcome.

The buyer wants:
- qualified meetings
- cleaner pipeline
- better-fit prospects
- faster follow-up
- less manual prospecting
- fewer tools to manage
- more visibility into what is working

Your offer should sound like a revenue system, not a technical build.

## Offer ladder

### Offer 1: AI Outbound Audit and Opportunity Map

Best for:
- first conversations
- low-friction entry offer
- diagnosing existing outbound
- warming up skeptical buyers

Deliverables:
- ICP review
- current outbound stack review
- lead source audit
- deliverability review
- CRM/pipeline leakage review
- 3–5 campaign opportunities
- recommended AI automation map
- quick-win action plan

Price:
- Beginner: ₹15,000–₹35,000
- Stronger once you have proof: ₹50,000–₹1,00,000

Positioning:
> In 7 days, we show exactly where your outbound pipeline is leaking and what AI-assisted workflows can fix first.

This is useful if the buyer is not ready for a retainer.

---

### Offer 2: AI Lead List and Personalization Sprint

Best for:
- beginner delivery
- one-time project
- clients who already have outreach tools
- fast proof of capability

Deliverables:
- define ICP and buying triggers
- build account list
- enrich decision makers
- verify emails
- add LinkedIn/company context
- create AI-personalized first lines or email angles
- segment leads by priority
- deliver CRM-ready CSV or upload into client tool

Price:
- ₹30,000–₹75,000 for a small sprint
- ₹75,000–₹1,50,000 for larger/complex niches

Possible volume:
- 250–500 researched leads for a beginner sprint
- 1,000–2,000 leads once workflow is mature

Positioning:
> We create a clean, enriched, segmented lead list your sales team can actually use — with AI-assisted research and personalization included.

This is the easiest beginner offer because it has limited moving parts and does not require you to guarantee meetings.

---

### Offer 3: AI Outbound Campaign Buildout

Best for:
- clients who need the system set up
- businesses with no SDR infrastructure
- higher perceived value than list building

Deliverables:
- ICP and offer refinement
- lead sourcing workflow
- enrichment workflow
- cold email infrastructure recommendations
- campaign copy
- personalization logic
- sequence setup
- reply routing
- CRM tagging
- reporting dashboard
- launch checklist

Price:
- Beginner: ₹75,000–₹1,50,000 setup
- With proof: ₹1,50,000–₹3,00,000 setup

Positioning:
> We build your AI-assisted outbound engine from scratch so you can go from scattered prospecting to a repeatable campaign system.

This is a strong core offer because it combines strategy, setup, and implementation.

---

### Offer 4: Done-for-You AI Outbound Retainer

Best for:
- recurring revenue
- companies that want meetings but not tool management
- agencies/services/SaaS with clear LTV

Deliverables:
- monthly prospect sourcing
- enrichment and verification
- campaign copy iterations
- AI personalization
- sending infrastructure monitoring
- weekly optimization
- reply triage
- booked meeting handoff process
- CRM updates/reporting

Price:
- Beginner: ₹75,000–₹1,50,000/month
- Strong delivery: ₹1,50,000–₹3,50,000/month
- Advanced/niche/proven results: ₹4,00,000+/month

Optional performance structure:
- setup fee + monthly retainer
- retainer + bonus per qualified meeting
- retainer + bonus per sales-qualified opportunity

Avoid pure pay-per-meeting at the beginning. It creates cash-flow risk and attracts clients with weak offers.

Positioning:
> We run your outbound engine every month — from lead research to personalized outreach to qualified meeting handoff.

---

### Offer 5: Speed-to-Lead AI Agent for Inbound Leads

Best for:
- companies already getting inbound leads
- paid ads agencies
- SaaS demos
- real estate/finance/education leads
- high-ticket service businesses

Deliverables:
- instant lead capture
- lead qualification
- enrichment
- routing to sales rep
- automated email/WhatsApp/SMS follow-up where compliant
- booking link flow
- CRM update
- no-show reminders

Price:
- ₹75,000–₹2,00,000 setup
- ₹50,000–₹2,00,000/month maintenance/optimization

Why it is strong:
- inbound leads are already warm
- ROI is easier to prove
- less deliverability risk than cold outbound

Positioning:
> We make sure every inbound lead gets researched, qualified, followed up, and routed before interest goes cold.

## Recommended beginner offer

Start with this:

> AI Outbound System Setup for B2B service businesses.

Package:
- 14-day setup
- ICP and offer workshop
- 500–1,000 researched prospects
- enriched decision makers
- 2–3 segmented cold email campaigns
- AI personalization workflow
- Instantly/Smartlead campaign setup
- reporting sheet/dashboard
- 30-day optimization add-on

Price:
- First 2–3 clients: ₹50,000–₹1,00,000 setup to build proof
- After proof: ₹1,50,000–₹2,50,000 setup
- Retainer add-on: ₹75,000–₹2,00,000/month

## What not to sell first

Avoid these as a beginner:
- “fully autonomous AI SDR that books meetings while you sleep”
- custom multi-agent software builds
- guaranteed meetings with no qualification boundaries
- complex enterprise RevOps transformations
- cold calling AI voice agents unless you understand compliance and call quality deeply
- scraping-heavy systems that may violate platform terms

## Pricing logic

Charge based on:
- value of one qualified meeting
- client’s average deal size
- sales cycle length
- how painful their current prospecting process is
- how much internal execution you replace
- complexity of data and personalization
- whether you run the system or only build it

Simple pricing anchor:
- If one client deal is worth ₹2L–₹10L+, charging ₹1L–₹3L for a pipeline system is reasonable.
- If one client deal is worth ₹20k–₹50k, outbound agency retainers will be harder to justify.
