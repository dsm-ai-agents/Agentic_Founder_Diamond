Category 1: B2B SaaS and Software Companies
1. I need more qualified demos from accounts that actually match our ICP, not a spreadsheet full of junior contacts who will never influence a software purchase.
2. I am spending too much on paid acquisition, and I need outbound or appointment-setting to lower CAC without filling the calendar with low-intent meetings.
3. I cannot rely only on founder referrals and inbound anymore; I need a repeatable pipeline engine that creates meetings every week, even when content or ads slow down.
4. I need leads who understand the problem we solve before they get on the call, because my sales team is wasting discovery time educating people from zero.
5. I am entering a crowded category, and I need outreach that creates urgency around our specific use case instead of sounding like another generic SaaS pitch.
6. I need meetings with the right buyer committee members, because selling to one enthusiastic user does not move the deal forward when finance, IT, security, or operations still need to approve.
7. I am worried that outsourced outreach will damage our domain reputation, so I need deliverability handled carefully with clean domains, warm-up, inbox rotation, and bounce controls.
8. I need reporting that connects outreach activity to pipeline and revenue, not just opens, replies, or booked calls that never become opportunities.
9. I need lead generation that can support longer enterprise sales cycles by nurturing accounts over time, not just chasing one-call closes that do not fit our ACV.
10. I need a way to test new verticals, job titles, and messaging quickly without hiring another SDR before I know which segment converts.
