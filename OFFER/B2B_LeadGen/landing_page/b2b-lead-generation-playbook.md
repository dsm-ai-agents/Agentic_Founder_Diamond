# The B2B Lead Generation Playbook

A practical, step-by-step system to book more meetings in the next 14 days

## Subtitle

Includes ICP checklist, lead list setup, cold outreach templates, and a follow-up sequence.

---

## Page 1 — What this playbook helps you do

If your outbound feels inconsistent, the issue is usually one of these:

- Targeting is too broad
- Messaging is not specific enough
- Follow-ups stop too early
- There’s no simple tracking system

This playbook gives you a lightweight system to fix all four—fast.

---

## Page 2 — The 3-Step System (overview)

Step 1: Target the right accounts (ICP clarity)

Step 2: Send a message that earns a reply (problem + outcome + proof)

Step 3: Follow up like a system (not “checking in”)

### Goal for the next 14 days

- Build a clean list of 200–500 leads
- Send 20–50 messages per day (depending on capacity)
- Run a 4–6 touch follow-up sequence
- Track reply rate + meetings booked

## Page 4 — Lead List Setup (quick and clean)

### Where to find leads

- LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- Company directories and associations
- Industry newsletters and event attendee lists
- BuiltWith / similar tech lookup tools

### Minimum fields to capture

- First name, last name
- Title
- Company
- Website
- LinkedIn URL
- Work email
- Phone (optional but valuable)
- Notes (trigger, tool used, recent post, etc.)

### Quality filter

Don’t add leads unless you can answer: “Why them, why now?”

## Page 5 — Message Framework (the only one you need)

Use this structure:

1. Personalization (1 line)
2. Problem (what they likely face)
3. Outcome (what they want)
4. Proof (how you know / what you’ve done)
5. CTA (simple, low-friction)

### Example

“Hey {{Name}} — saw {{relevant trigger}}.

Many {{role}} teams struggle with {{pain}} which leads to {{cost}}.

We help {{ICP}} achieve {{outcome}} in {{timeframe}} using {{method}}.

Open to a quick 10-minute chat to see if this fits?”

## Page 6 — Cold Email Templates (copy/paste)

### Template A — simple + direct

**Subject:** Quick question about {{Company}}

Hi {{Name}},

Noticed {{trigger}} at {{Company}}.

Are you currently working on {{relevant initiative}}?

We help {{ICP}} get {{outcome}} by {{method}}.

If it’s useful, I can share a 1-page plan for {{Company}}.

Should I send it over?

Thanks,  
{{Your Name}}

### Template B — value first

**Subject:** {{Outcome}} for {{Company}}?

Hi {{Name}},

I put together a quick checklist we use to help {{ICP}} improve {{outcome}}:

- {{point 1}}
- {{point 2}}
- {{point 3}}

If you tell me what you’re using for {{tool/process}} today, I can tailor it to {{Company}}.

Worth sharing?

— {{Your Name}}

## Page 7 — LinkedIn DM Templates

### Connection request (no pitch)

“Hey {{Name}} — liked your post on {{topic}}. I work with {{ICP}} on {{outcome}}. Would love to connect.”

### After they accept

“Thanks for connecting, {{Name}}. Quick question—are you currently focused on {{initiative}} at {{Company}}?”

### If they reply “yes”

“Got it. We typically see {{pain}} show up when teams do {{context}}. If helpful, I can share the exact sequence we use to book meetings in {{industry}}. Want it?”

## Page 8 — Follow-up Sequence (4–6 touches)

Most meetings come from follow-ups. Use this:

### Touch 1 — Day 1: Initial message

Send your first cold email or LinkedIn message.

### Touch 2 — Day 2: Add proof

“Quick add—recently helped {{similar company}} get {{result}} in {{timeframe}}. Worth exploring?”

### Touch 3 — Day 4: Offer asset

“I can share a short checklist/template for {{outcome}}—want it?”

### Touch 4 — Day 7: Clarify fit

“Before I close this out—are you the right person for {{topic}}, or should I speak to someone else?”

### Touch 5 — Day 10: Breakup message

“Looks like timing isn’t right. If you want, I can send the playbook and you can reach out when it’s a priority.”

---

## Page 9 — KPI Tracker (keep it simple)

Track weekly:

- New leads added
- Messages sent
- Replies
- Positive replies
- Meetings booked
- Cost per meeting (if running ads/tools)

### Benchmarks (guideline)

- Cold outreach reply rate: 3–10%
- Positive reply rate: 1–5%
- Meetings booked depend on offer clarity + targeting

## Page 10 — Your 14-Day Execution Plan

Day 1–2: Finalize ICP + offer + messaging

Day 3–4: Build list of 200–500 leads

Day 5–14: Run daily outreach + follow-ups

### Daily checklist

- Add 20–50 leads
- Send 20–50 messages
- Follow up with yesterday’s non-responders
- Track replies + meetings booked
