FAQ

Common questions before you start

Do you guarantee meetings?

No. We guarantee delivery of a researched, measurable outbound system. Meeting volume depends on your offer, market, list quality, deliverability, and sales follow-up.

Can you use our existing tools?

Yes. We can work with tools such as Apollo, Clay, Instantly, Smartlead, HubSpot, Airtable, or Google Sheets if they fit your workflow.

Who writes the outreach?

We create segmented message angles and sequences. Your team reviews and approves brand-sensitive copy before launch.

What do you need from us?

We need your ICP, offer details, CRM/outreach setup where relevant, sales feedback, and timely approvals.

What happens after 30 days?

You can continue with a managed retainer, run the system internally, or add optimization / lead-volume support.

Is this only cold email?

No. The core system is outbound-first, but the same research and segmentation can support LinkedIn, event follow-up, ABM, and inbound speed-to-lead workflows.
