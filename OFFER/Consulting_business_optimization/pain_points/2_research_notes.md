Research synthesis focus:
- Buyers looking for AI/business process optimization consulting are usually not buying “AI agents” directly.
- They are looking for measurable improvements in pipeline, meetings booked, CAC, lead quality, conversion, compliance, deliverability, sales cycle length, team capacity, and reporting accuracy.
- The three broad categories selected are practical beginner-friendly entry points with repeated workflows and clear ROI.
