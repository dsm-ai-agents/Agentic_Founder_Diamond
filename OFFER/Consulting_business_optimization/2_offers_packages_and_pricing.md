# 2. Offers, Packages, and Pricing

## Core principle

Do not sell “AI agents.” Sell a business process result.

Bad:
> I build AI agents for your business.

Better:
> I help service businesses reduce manual admin and lead follow-up delays by redesigning and automating their most repetitive workflows.

## Offer ladder

A beginner-friendly consulting business should have a simple offer ladder:

1. Low-risk audit
2. Fixed-scope implementation
3. Monthly optimization/support
4. Higher-ticket transformation project

This makes it easier for a client to start small and then expand.

## Package 1: AI Process Opportunity Audit

### Best for

Business owners/operators who know they are inefficient but do not know what to automate first.

### Promise

Identify the top 3 automation opportunities, estimate ROI, and create a practical implementation roadmap.

### Deliverables

- 60-90 minute discovery call
- Process inventory across sales, operations, admin, support, finance, or HR
- Pain-point scoring: time wasted, error rate, revenue impact, frequency, automation feasibility
- Tool stack review
- Automation opportunity map
- Top 3 recommended workflows
- ROI estimate
- 30-day implementation roadmap

### Price range

Beginner:
- India/local SMB: ₹15,000-₹50,000
- International SMB: $300-$1,000

As proof improves:
- India/local SMB: ₹50,000-₹1,50,000
- International SMB: $1,000-$3,000

### Sales angle

> Before you spend money on AI tools, let’s find the process where automation will actually pay back.

## Package 2: Workflow Automation Sprint

### Best for

Companies with one clear bottleneck.

### Promise

Automate one painful workflow in 2-4 weeks.

### Example workflows

- Website lead → CRM → qualification email → sales notification → follow-up reminders
- Sales call transcript → summary → CRM update → proposal draft
- New customer onboarding → forms → task creation → document checklist → email sequence
- Support inbox triage → category → suggested reply → escalation
- Invoice reminder workflow
- Weekly KPI reporting from multiple tools

### Deliverables

- Current-state process map
- Future-state process design
- Automation build using Zapier/Make/n8n/Power Automate/custom scripts where appropriate
- AI prompt/system instructions where needed
- QA testing
- Loom walkthrough
- SOP/documentation
- 1 training session
- 14-30 days of bug fixing

### Price range

Beginner:
- India/local SMB: ₹50,000-₹2,00,000
- International SMB: $1,500-$5,000

With stronger proof:
- India/local/mid-market: ₹2,00,000-₹8,00,000+
- International SMB/mid-market: $5,000-$20,000+

## Package 3: AI Operations Setup for a Department

### Best for

Businesses that want multiple workflows improved across one function.

### Target departments

- Sales operations
- Customer support
- Recruiting/HR operations
- Finance/admin
- Agency client onboarding
- Healthcare clinic admin
- Real estate lead management

### Promise

Turn one department into a more automated, measurable operating system.

### Deliverables

- Department process audit
- Process redesign
- 3-5 automations
- AI assistant/agent for one internal use case
- Dashboard/reporting setup
- SOP library
- Team training
- Governance rules: approvals, human-in-the-loop steps, error handling
- 30-60 days support

### Price range

- India/local SMB: ₹2,50,000-₹10,00,000
- International SMB/mid-market: $7,500-$30,000+

## Package 4: Monthly AI Ops Partner / Retainer

### Best for

Clients who already completed an implementation and need ongoing improvements.

### Promise

Keep improving workflows, fixing automations, and identifying new efficiency gains every month.

### Deliverables

- Monthly workflow review
- Automation monitoring
- Prompt/workflow improvements
- Minor new automations
- Team office hours
- KPI report
- Documentation updates

### Price range

Beginner:
- India/local SMB: ₹30,000-₹1,00,000/month
- International SMB: $750-$2,500/month

With stronger results:
- India/mid-market: ₹1,00,000-₹5,00,000/month
- International: $2,500-$10,000/month

## Package 5: AI Agent Build

This should not be your first offer unless the use case is very clear.

### Best for

Clients who already have clean processes/data and need an agentic workflow.

### Example agents

- Sales research agent
- CRM hygiene agent
- Support triage agent
- Proposal drafting agent
- Recruiting screening assistant
- Internal knowledge assistant
- Meeting-to-action-items agent

### Deliverables

- Use-case design
- Input/output schema
- Tool integrations
- Guardrails
- Human approval steps
- Testing dataset
- Deployment
- Monitoring plan

### Price range

- Simple agentic workflow: ₹1,00,000-₹4,00,000 / $3,000-$10,000
- Multi-tool production workflow: ₹4,00,000-₹15,00,000+ / $10,000-$50,000+

## Pricing rationale

You can price based on:

1. Time saved
2. Error reduction
3. Faster response time
4. Increased lead conversion
5. Reduced need for hiring
6. Faster cash collection
7. Improved management visibility

Example framing:

> If a ₹1,50,000 project saves 40 hours/month of team time, reduces missed leads, and avoids one additional admin hire, the ROI is easy to justify.

## Beginner pricing strategy

For the first 3-5 clients:

- Do not underprice the audit too much; charge something so the buyer is serious.
- Keep implementation scope narrow.
- Offer a “pilot workflow” rather than a full transformation.
- Ask for permission to create a case study.
- Measure before/after metrics.
- Avoid unlimited support.

Recommended first offer:

> AI Process Opportunity Audit + One Workflow Automation Sprint

Suggested launch pricing:

- Audit: ₹25,000-₹50,000, or $750-$1,500 for international SMBs
- Sprint: ₹75,000-₹2,00,000, or $3,000-$5,000 for international SMBs
- Retainer: ₹40,000-₹1,00,000/month after implementation, or $1,000-$2,500/month internationally

Subagent pricing benchmark from tool/vendor research:

- AI/process automation audit: $500-$2,500
- Automation roadmap sprint: $2,500-$7,500
- Quick-win automation build: $3,000-$10,000
- AI chatbot/knowledge assistant MVP: $5,000-$20,000
- AI agent/workflow pilot: $10,000-$35,000
- Monthly optimization retainer: $1,000-$5,000/month
- Custom AI/software MVP: often $50,000+; ITRex estimates AI MVPs commonly start around $50k: https://itrexgroup.com/blog/how-much-does-artificial-intelligence-cost/

## Important warning

Never promise “replace your employees with AI.” That creates fear, resistance, and operational risk.

Position as:

> AI-assisted operations that remove repetitive work and give your team more capacity.
